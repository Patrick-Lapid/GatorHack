At Verizon, we offer a variety of Apple Watch models (like the all-new Apple Watch Series 7), Android smartwatches, and beyond. We also sell watch accessories, like watch bands, watch screen protectors, watch chargers, and more so you can always make sure your device is charged, protected, and accessorized to your liking.Verizon also has a variety of styles of smartwatches perfect for both men, women and even kids, meaning you can shop a variety of products that suit your particular style and taste. So, which smartwatch is right for you?Which smartwatch should you buy?The best smartwatch for you depends on the devices you plan to pair it with, the style you're looking for, as well as size and a few other key factors. The Apple Watch Series 7, for example, comes in a variety of colors and two different sizes, and pairs seamlessly with your iPhone 6S or later with the most recent iOS version. What's more, Verizon has a variety of Apple Watch Bands you can shop, so you can make sure your Apple Watch looks stylish with any outfit you choose to pair it with.Android smartwatches also pair to different Android devices, such as the Samsung Galaxy Watch4, which pairs with your Android device operating on Android 6 or higher. Samsung Galaxy Watch4 and Samsung Galaxy Watch4 Classic also come in a variety of colors, and the Samsung Galaxy Watch4 Classic offers two sizing options as well, so you can choose how large you want your display to be. Connected smartwatches can help you stay on track with everything from your health and wellness to your texts, emails, and beyond, and look stylish while doing it. No matter which you choose, Verizon has a variety of smartwatches and smartwatch accessories to browse now, both in-store and online.
